How It Works

Three simple steps to automate your entire workflow on Google Flow AI.

1

Enter Your Prompts

Paste a list of text prompts into the extension sidebar. One prompt per line, unlimited with Pro.

2

Auto Generate

Click start and the extension automatically submits each prompt to Flow AI with smart wait times.

3

Scan & Download

The smart scanner finds all generated images. Download them all at once into organized folders.
